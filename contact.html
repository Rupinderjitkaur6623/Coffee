Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes! We accept reservations for groups of 6 or more. For smaller groups, we operate on a first-come, first-served basis. You can make a reservation by calling us or using our contact form.

Do you offer catering services?

Absolutely! We provide catering services for events, meetings, and special occasions. Our catering menu includes coffee, pastries, sandwiches, and more. Contact us for a custom quote.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a small parking lot behind the café, and there's also street parking available. We're also easily accessible by public transportation.

Do you have WiFi?

Yes, we offer free WiFi for our customers. It's perfect for working, studying, or just browsing while enjoying your coffee.

Are you pet-friendly?

We love pets! Well-behaved dogs are welcome in our outdoor seating area. We even have water bowls available for your furry friends.
