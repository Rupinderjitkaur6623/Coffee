Our Story

From a small dream to your favorite neighborhood café

The CozyCup Journey

Founded in 2018, CozyCup Café began as a simple dream to create a space where people could enjoy exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming atmosphere. What started as a small corner shop has grown into a beloved community gathering place.

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, spent years traveling the world learning about coffee culture and perfecting the art of brewing. She brought back not just recipes, but the philosophy that coffee is more than a drink—it's an experience that brings people together.

Today, CozyCup Café continues to serve our community with the same passion and dedication that inspired us from day one. We source the finest beans, train our baristas to craft the perfect cup, and create an environment where every visit feels like coming home.
